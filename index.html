Team Structure

Two Tracks.
One Standard.

Scholars Elite operates an Academy track and a Select/National track at each age level. Placement is based on readiness — not age alone. We build teams to develop players, not collect fees.

Track One

Academy

Development-first environment. Focused on skill acquisition, habits, and readiness. Athletes who need structure before competition. Not a lesser path — the correct path for building confidence and competence.

Track Two

Select / National

Competitive teams for athletes prepared for higher-level competition and evaluation. Roster spots are earned. Expectations are high. Movement between tracks is intentional and performance-based.

MS

Scholars Elite Middle School

This is the foundation stage of the Scholars Elite pipeline. The focus is teaching athletes how to train, how to compete, and how to learn. No recruiting pressure. No exposure chase. Just the habits and fundamentals that define a career.

Middle school athletes are coached directly by Middle School Director Bernie Hynson, whose primary mission is long-term development — not short-term results. Development-appropriate competition, controlled environments, and confidence built through correct reps.

Fundamental SkillsFootwork & PositioningDecision-MakingDefensive HabitsAccountabilityConfidence Building

Track One
Academy

Scholars Elite Academy / Developmental

The Academy track exists for athletes who need structured development before competitive exposure. This is not a lesser path — it is the correct path for athletes who want to build confidence, skill, and consistency before the pressure of national competition.

Technical skill rebuilding or refinement. Strength, movement, coordination. Game understanding and spacing. Accountability habits. Athletes move into Select teams when readiness is demonstrated — not on a timeline, not because of age. Because they earned it.

Technical Skill RefinementMovement & CoordinationGame UnderstandingAccountability HabitsConfidence BuildingNo Recruiting Pressure

RECRUITING CONTEXT: Preparation only. No timelines forced. Athletes advance to Select when readiness is demonstrated.

Movement Between Tracks

Placement is not static. Athletes may move Academy → Select → National. Movement is based on skill progression, coachability, consistency, and accountability. We reward development — not entitlement.
